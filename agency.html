SDSU Cryptocurrency Club

The newest club at SDSU

The San Diego State University Cryptocurrency Club (SDSUCC) was formed in 2017 as a result of the increasing popularity of cryptocurrencies and blockchain applications. The SDSUCC is open to all students and non-students with an interest in cryptocurrency from financial and technical perspectives. Whether you just heard about Bitcoin or you programmed your own smart contracts, SDSUCC is designed to educate and discuss topics such as forks, security and cryptographic hashes, etc.

Meeting Times

Check our Google Calendar listed below. We try to have all the blockchain-related talks and conferences in San Diego and Orange County listed on our calendar so feel free to add it to your calendar.

Technology

With the vast amount of research out there, we hope to discuss topics such as the adoption of blockchain technology, and smart contracts. We also hope to conduct smart contract workshops and hold talks from blockchain engineers.

Finance

With the rise of trading platforms, ICOs, and news featuring cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum, we also hope to discuss the financial aspect of cryptocurrencies.

Research

We want to educate students, faculty, and members of the community on the important applications of blockchain technology. By holding discussion platforms where people can discuss and debate about the latest projects at the same time maintaining high standards of research, we hope to grow in our knowledge and understanding of the disruptive effects of blockchain.
